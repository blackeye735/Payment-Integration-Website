About Us:

The Sparks Foundation is a non-profit organisation which believes in its delicacy and kindness by providing food and shelter to the needy children.It has been working towards the motto of making India free from hunger and starvation for 20 long years. The children who beg in the streets, who starve for a single lump of rice....we assure them a way to live, to survive in their difficulties.
The motto is "Supporting hungry, Protesting against hunger"
